ℹ ABOUT
Ball Bounce is an endless vertical scroller.

A ball falls constantly under gravity.
Draw lines with your mouse (or finger) to create
temporary platforms that bounce the ball upward.

Collect ✦ gems to replenish your drawing fuel.
The higher you go, the higher your score.

If the ball falls off the bottom — it's game over.

Built with JavaScript · planck.js · MongoDB
