ℹ ABOUT
Ball Bounce is an endless vertical scroller.

A ball falls constantly under gravity.
Draw lines with your mouse (or finger) to create
temporary platforms that bounce the ball upward.

Collect ✦ gems to replenish your drawing fuel.
The higher you go, the higher your score.

If the ball falls off the bottom — it's game over.

Built with JavaScript · planck.js · MongoDB

❓ HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE

🎯Bounce the ball as high as possible without letting it fall off the bottom of the screen.
📈Your score is based on height reached. The higher you climb, the higher your score.

CONTROLS

✏️Draw a line with your mouse (or finger on mobile) to create a temporary platform. The ball bounces off it upward.
⛽Every line you draw costs fuel. Longer lines cost more fuel. You cannot draw without fuel.
⏱️Platforms fade and disappear after a few seconds — plan ahead!

FUEL

🔋You start with 100 fuel out of a maximum of 500. Collect gems to replenish or lose fuel.
⚠️If you run out of fuel you cannot draw any more platforms — the ball will eventually fall.

LEVELS

⬆️The game has 50 levels. As your score increases, gravity and ball speed gradually increase, making the game harder.
🔴At higher levels platforms also disappear faster — react quickly!

GEMS

YELLOW — Circle+10 fuel  ·  Most common (60%)
RED — Triangle−10 fuel  ·  Penalty gem (20%)
GREEN — Diamond+25 fuel  ·  Uncommon (15%)
BLUE — Star⚡ 3.3× speed boost for 5 seconds (decays gradually)  ·  Rare (5%)

TIPS

💡Draw short lines — they cost less fuel and are often more effective than long ones.
💡Angle your platforms to redirect the ball toward gems or away from the edges.
💡Avoid red gems when your fuel is already low — they can strand you.
💡Blue boost launches the ball fast — have a platform ready to catch it on the way down!
